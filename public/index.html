FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EscaraPay's payment protection system

What is the minimum token amount on EscaraPay?
The minimum token is ₹200. It is a percentage of the order value that is held securely in our protected vault until delivery is confirmed.
What if the seller doesn't ship the item?
If the seller fails to ship within the agreed time, the full token amount is automatically refunded to the buyer. No questions asked.
When does the seller receive the token payment?
The payment is released to the seller within 7 days of delivery confirmation, or immediately when the buyer manually confirms receipt.
Is EscaraPay RBI compliant and secure?
EscaraPay processes payments via Cashfree, which is fully RBI-compliant and PCI DSS Level 1 certified, ensuring maximum security for every transaction in India.
How do I raise a dispute on EscaraPay?
Within 7 days of delivery, go to your dashboard and click 'Raise Dispute'. Our team will investigate and respond within 24 hours with a fair, evidence-based resolution.
Are there any hidden charges on EscaraPay?
No hidden charges. Only a 2% Cashfree gateway fee is applied, which is shown transparently at the time of payment. No monthly fees, no setup fees.
How does EscaraPay protect WhatsApp sellers from RTO losses?
EscaraPay requires buyers to pay a minimum ₹200 token before the seller ships the product. If the buyer cancels after dispatch causing an RTO, this token amount is transferred to the seller as compensation, covering shipping and handling costs.
Can Instagram and WhatsApp sellers use EscaraPay?
Yes! EscaraPay is specifically designed for WhatsApp and Instagram sellers in India. Create a secure payment link, share it directly via WhatsApp chat or Instagram DM, and protect every transaction from fraud and RTO.
